About Speak Eazy

Speak Eazy is an innovative application designed to bridge the communication gap for individuals using sign language. With advanced recognition technology, our app translates sign language into text and speech, making communication easier and more accessible for everyone.

Features

  • Real-time sign language recognition
  • Text and voice translation
  • User-friendly interface
  • Supports multiple sign languages
